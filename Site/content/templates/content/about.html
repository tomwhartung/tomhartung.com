Corn-Pone Opinions

Twain's essay is about a slave he heard talking to himself about how people think they are thinking for themselves but in reality are just regurgitatingthe opinions they get where they get their corn pone.

Corn pone is used to make corn bread and it was a basic food back then.

The essay is only a few pages long. You can probably find a pdf of it online fairly quickly.

Election results show this still rings true

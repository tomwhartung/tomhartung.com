In Search of Accuracy

If accuracy is important to you, definitely seek out and take other quizzes, to get the opinions different quiz creators.

A significant consideration is, we all — quiz takers and creators alike — have our own biases. Ultimately it is up to you to decide what your preferences are.

Speaking from personal experience, I'd suggest you might want to be aware of others who may be influencing your sense of self. Your answers — and hence your sense of who you are — may even be unconsiously influenced by the expections of society as a whole. Worse, these preferences may actually be subconscious, having been internalized by decades of experiences in your day-to-day life in whatever culture in which you happen to find yourself.

Finally, observing others and even learning to be mindful can help you understand any external and internal forces you may be experiencing, but these topics are far outside the scope of this disclaimer.

Put simply, an online quiz can do only so much, the important thing is: to thine own self be true.

Saving Your Results

The SeeOurMinds.com quiz allows you to store your quiz results on our server under your email address. Once you save your answers, the quiz page enables you to retrieve, change, and add to them when you return.

Note that using your email address to save your quiz results does not sign you up for the Artsy Visions Monthly Newsletter. To sign up for our newsletter, use one of the forms on the site that is labeled specifically for that purpose.

We save your results on our server only if you specifically request we do so, by providing an email address to save them under.

Why Save Your Results?

The ability to save your results on our server exists solely for your convenience.

Saving them allows you to, for example, save your answers for a shorter quiz and come back later and answer additional questions. This feature also lets you go back and change a few answers to see how that might effect the score. Finally, saving them also means you can access your results at any time without needing to write them down.
